## Phase 1: The Credit Card Sweep
*Export your last 90 days of expenses. Filter for recurring charges < $100.*

- [ ] **Dropbox / Box / iCloud**
    - *Check:* Are users storing company data on personal drives?
    - *Fix:* Migrate to OneDrive/SharePoint.
- [ ] **Adobe Creative Cloud**
    - *Check:* Does this user *actually* use Photoshop, or just view PDFs?
    - *Fix:* Downgrade to Acrobat Standard or use built-in PDF viewers.
- [ ] **Zoom / Webex**
    - *Check:* Do you already pay for Microsoft Teams or Google Meet?
    - *Fix:* Cancel redundant video conferencing licenses.
- [ ] **Canva Pro**
    - *Check:* Are 5 people paying for individual Pro accounts?
    - *Fix:* Consolidate into a generic "Marketing@" Team account.

## Phase 2: The Offboarding Gap
*Check the status of employees who left in the last 12 months.*

- [ ] **Microsoft 365 Licenses**
    - [ ] Converted ex-employees to "Shared Mailboxes" (Free)?
    - [ ] Reclaimed E3/Business Premium licenses?
- [ ] **Salesforce / CRM**
    - [ ] Deactivated users? (Salesforce charges for active seats).
- [ ] **Password Managers**
    - [ ] Revoked access to LastPass/1Password?

## Phase 3: Infrastructure
- [ ] **Domain Registrars (GoDaddy/Namecheap)**
    - [ ] Are auto-renewals on for domains you no longer use?
- [ ] **Web Hosting**
    - [ ] Are you paying for a Wix site that was replaced by your new site?
